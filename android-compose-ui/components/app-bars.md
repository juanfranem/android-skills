# App Bars

## Use when

- The screen needs a consistent place for title, navigation, and high-frequency actions.

## Variants

- `TopAppBar`
- `CenterAlignedTopAppBar`
- `MediumTopAppBar`
- `LargeTopAppBar`
- `BottomAppBar`

## Scroll behavior

- `enterAlwaysScrollBehavior`
- `exitUntilCollapsedScrollBehavior`
- `pinnedScrollBehavior`

## Guidance

- Use small or center-aligned top bars for simpler screens.
- Use medium or large top bars when title presence and scroll behavior matter.
- Pair with `Scaffold`.

## Key APIs

- `navigationIcon`
- `actions`
- `scrollBehavior`
- `colors`
