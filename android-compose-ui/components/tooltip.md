# Tooltip

## Use when

- A compact control needs a short explanatory label or contextual hint.

## Variants

- `PlainTooltip`
- `RichTooltip`
- `TooltipBox`

## Guidance

- Plain tooltips fit terse labels.
- Rich tooltips fit slightly deeper explanation and optional affordances.
- Do not make tooltip the only path to understand a critical action.

## Common mistakes

- Hiding essential guidance behind hover/long-press only.
